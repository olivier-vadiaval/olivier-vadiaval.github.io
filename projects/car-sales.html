Tableau Viz: Car Sales

Technologies used: Excel, Tableau
Dashboard/Tableau Viz: Car Sales Dashboard by Olivier Vadiavaloo,
Dataset: Car Sales

In this project, I create a dashboard (or Viz) in Tableau from car sales data (source: Kaggle Datasets) cleaned in Microsoft Excel. The data contains the manufacturer name, sales price, the resale price, the number of units sold, the engine size, the horsepower and more for each car model.
